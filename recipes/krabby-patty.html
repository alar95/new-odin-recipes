The Krabby Patty

Description:

This page will guide you in making the crowing achievement brought to you by the krusty krab, the krabby patty. A burger so amazing that even a rival competitor has made many attempts to steal the sacred recipe but no longer will you have to wonder as i am about to show you what ingredients you will need and the steps to take, but be careful and quiet otherwise plankton might just come knocking on your door so ssshhhh!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Sesame Seed Bun
  • 1 Qaurter Ounce Beef Patty
  • Some Iceburg Lettuce
  • American Cheese Slices
  • Tomato Slices
  • Sliced Pickles
  • Sliced Onion
  • Ketchup
  • Musturd

Steps to take:

  1. Season the beef patty with salt and pepper
  2. place on a cast iron skillet for 4 mintues
  3. then flip and cook for another 4 minutes
  4. When fully cooked remove from skillet to rest for 30 seconds before following the presentation steps below

Presentation of Krabby Patty

  1. Bottom Bun
  2. Beef Patty
  3. Iceburg Lettuce
  4. Cheese
  5. Onion
  6. Tomato
  7. Ketchup
  8. Mustard
  9. Pickles
  10. Top Bun

There you have it, your very own Krabby Patty! Enjoy!
